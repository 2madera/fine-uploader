Support


For technical support questions, please use Stack Overflow. The Fine Uploader development team monitors the "fine-uploader" tag for new questions, and will provide assistance shortly after you post your question.


Please look for existing questions under the "fine-uploader" tag that match your question. If you don't find any, please post a new question tagged with "fine-uploader".

You may also want to take a peek at the now-closed Fine Uploader support forum on GetSatisfaction for a match to your question. Again, if no match is found in GetSatisfaction or on Stack Overflow, please post a new question on Stack Overflow tagged with "fine-uploader". Questions that do not directly relate to Fine Uploader (such as general javascript, CSS, HTML, HTTP, or server-side language questions) can also be posted in Stack Overflow, but not with the "fine-uploader" tag.


Only bugs and feature requests should be reported in the Fine Uploader Github project's issue tracker.
